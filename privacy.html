Our Secret Recipe Policy 🥟

At Momo Hub, we treat your privacy like our secret momo filling: Protected, safe, and never shared with random strangers.

🥬 The Ingredients We Collect

We only collect the "ingredients" necessary to serve you perfectly:

  • Your Name: So we don't call you "Hey You".
  • Phone Number: To call you when your order is steaming hot.
  • Location: To ensure our delivery partners (Zomato) find you.

🔥 How We Cook Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Processing your delicious orders.
  • Improving our website (making it tastier).
  • Sending you occasional spicy updates (if you ask for them).

🍪 Cookies (Not the Tasty Kind)

Our website uses digital cookies. Sadly, these are not edible. They help our website run smoothly and remember your preferences. You can turn them off in your browser, but the website might taste a bit bland.

🥡 Sharing with Others

We do not sell your data. The only people who see your info are:

  • Zomato: If you click "Order", they handle the delivery.
  • Google: To show you our map and reviews.

🔒 The Steamer Lid (Security)

We keep your data tightly sealed under a heavy steamer lid. We use standard security measures to protect your information from getting cold or stolen.
